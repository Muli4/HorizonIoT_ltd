Who We Are

Bridging technology and innovation through intelligent IoT & GPS solutions across East Africa.

Horizon IoT Ltd is a leading GPS hardware aggregator in East Africa, delivering a comprehensive suite of GPS-based devices, video telematics, and accessories for location tracking and asset management.

We act as a bridge between hardware manufacturers and system integrators, offering a seamless ecosystem of high-end IoT devices and software solutions that empower businesses to monitor, manage, and protect their assets in real-time.

HOW OUR TELEMATICS SOLUTIONS EASE IT FOR YOU

Enhanced Fleet Visibility

  • Real-Time Tracking: Monitor vehicle locations in real time, ensuring you always know where your assets are.
  • Historical Data: Access historical routes and usage patterns to make informed decisions.
