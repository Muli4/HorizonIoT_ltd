About Horizon IoT

Horizon IoT Ltd is a leading provider of cutting-edge telematics and Internet of Things (IoT) solutions across East Africa and beyond. We specialize in providing real-time tracking, intelligent data analytics, and connected automation that empower businesses to operate efficiently, securely, and sustainably.

Our comprehensive portfolio includes GPS tracking, fuel management, asset monitoring, and driver behavior analysis — all integrated into one seamless IoT ecosystem designed to simplify and optimize fleet operations.

Vehicle Trackers

Reliable GPS tracking devices like Teltonika and Concox for real-time location monitoring, geofencing, and route optimization.

Fuel Sensors

Advanced Escort TD150 BLE and TD BLE sensors to monitor fuel levels, detect pilferage, and ensure precise fuel usage analytics.

Dash Cameras & MDVR

Dual-view cameras and mobile DVR systems for driver accountability, safety recording, and fleet surveillance in real time.

Asset Tracking

Portable asset tracking devices with long battery life for containers, cargo, and valuable equipment monitoring anywhere, anytime.

IoT Solutions

Smart sensors and telemetry modules integrated into a single cloud platform — bringing automation and control to your entire operation.

Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive dashboards that visualize driver behavior, fleet performance, and fuel trends for smarter, data-driven decisions.
